GAAPP (Gateway Adventure App) is an interactive platform for creating, managing, and participating in immersive adventures, scavenger hunts, escape room experiences, educational activities, and location-based games.

About GAAPP

GAAPP helps creators build engaging adventure experiences for players of all ages. Whether you're organizing a community event, educational challenge, team-building activity, or escape-room style game, GAAPP provides the tools needed to design and manage interactive experiences.

Participants can access adventures, complete challenges, solve puzzles, and track their progress through the platform.

Features

Create Adventures

Design custom scavenger hunts, escape rooms, and interactive challenges.

Manage Participants

Organize players, teams, and event participants.

Track Progress

Monitor challenge completion and player achievements.

Share Experiences

Publish and distribute adventures to your audience.
